# Completion And Source-System Updates

Recompute completion from current owner evidence; never infer it from conversation momentum or historical plan exhaustion.

For each Leg require:

- source-system-backed Work Item dispositions;
- current integrated behavioral evidence across its real seams;
- completed independent Clerk checks;
- resolved or correctly routed Findings;
- verified cleanup or explicit unsettled failure;
- current commit/PR/check/deployment/Runtime locators as applicable.

For the Deliver session require:

- the bound Route revision remains valid;
- every required Leg is completed or has an Architect-accepted disposition;
- Blueprint Contract and Route coverage obligations remain satisfied;
- no unresolved Finding undermines Route, Blueprint, Leg, or Destination claims;
- configured tracker/Delivery Map projections are updated from their owning source facts;
- limitations, deferred production transitions, and follow-up remain visible;
- required landing/merge/deployment/publication authority is explicit.

Updating a configured tracker or Delivery Map is a projection/update through its owner, not authority transfer to Deliver. Do not mark external systems complete unless their own evidence supports it.

Record `completed`, `route-invalidated`, `failed`, or `cancelled` truthfully. Human confirmation is required where the configured workflow or Destination acceptance requires it.
