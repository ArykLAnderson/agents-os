# Independent Verification

A Clerk independently checks each Work Item and integrated Leg candidate. The Clerk did not author the work it judges and does not coordinate its implementation.

Check against:

- accepted Blueprint Contracts and Route acceptance evidence;
- immediate consumer sufficiency;
- integrated behavioral movement across real seams;
- required negative, failure, compatibility, migration, and recovery paths;
- source/worktree and effect boundaries;
- temporary-resource cleanup and retained-state truthfulness;
- exact commands/evidence actually run and read;
- current source-system locators.

Classify output as Findings, not authority:

- conformance/workmanship defect suitable for local repair;
- evidence gap under unchanged semantics;
- suspected material Route invalidation;
- behavior/architecture/Route proposal requiring the owning capability;
- governance proposal requiring explicit acceptance;
- mistaken or out of scope.

The Marshal may challenge evidence or classification with counterevidence but cannot overrule, suppress, or rewrite a Clerk Finding. Unresolved material disagreement routes to the Architect or owning semantic capability.
