# Integrate A Leg Candidate

Integration is semantic and source-backed. It joins completed Work Item contributions at the Route's named convergence seam and proves the resulting branch/system remains coherent.

Before integration:

- every included Work Item has a source locator, seam-local evidence, and independent disposition;
- exported Contracts are consumer-sufficient;
- prerequisite facts still hold;
- temporary compatibility mechanisms and cleanup ownership are explicit;
- no unresolved Finding already invalidates the Leg or Route.

At integration:

- use the configured source workflow and one integration owner;
- reconcile competing contributions against Blueprint Contracts rather than choosing mechanically;
- run the Route-specified integrated evidence across real crossed seams;
- preserve source-system history/locators and avoid copying diffs or logs into Deliver state;
- treat merge success as mechanics, not behavioral proof.

A Leg candidate cannot pass by depending on a later Leg, substituting mocks for crossed seams, or deferring all meaningful evidence to the final Route Leg.
