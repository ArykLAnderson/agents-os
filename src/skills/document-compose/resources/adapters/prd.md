# PRD Composition Adapter

Use this adapter for a product reader who must decide whether a problem, outcome, boundary, and validation plan are ready to become implementation work.

**Structure mode:** recommended. Preserve the reader obligations without imposing a universal section template.

## Reader Contract

- Name the product or delivery decision the reader can make from this document.
- Lead with the user or operational problem, intended outcome, and bounded request. Do not turn a draft into an approval or implementation authorization.
- Separate accepted requirements from observations, proposals, open questions, and explicitly deferred work.

## Selection And Gaps

- Select intent, decision, scope, constraint, success signal, and material risk entries. Preserve each Case identity when more than one Case contributes.
- Record selected but omitted entries, including why the reader can safely proceed without them.
- Block composition when an essential user, owner, acceptance, safety, or decision boundary is absent. A useful PRD may name that gap; it may not invent the missing requirement.
- Recommend `review-briefing` when the next reader action is bounded review. Use a different strategy when the reader first needs concepts taught.

## Draft Basis

Produce a manifest and draft basis that make these distinctions visible: current requirement, evidence, dependency, open decision, and out-of-scope work. Every proposed success measure must be Case-backed or visibly qualified as not yet specified.

Route a proposed requirement, changed scope, new success criterion, or authority conflict to `document-reconcile`.
