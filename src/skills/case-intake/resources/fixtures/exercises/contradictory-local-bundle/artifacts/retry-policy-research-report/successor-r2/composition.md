# Successor Research Report Composition

## Reader Contract

An RFC reviewer needs to decide whether the RFC may state a current migration retry count after the baseline capacity support changed. The report must show the difference between evidence invalidating a report claim and author approval superseding a Case decision.

## Draft Basis

- Direct the reader not to state a retry count.
- Explain that `OBS-004` contradicts `OBS-003` and invalidates the baseline report's four-retry capacity support.
- Explain that `APR-004` separately supersedes `DEC-002` with `DEC-003`; the rerun does not exercise policy authority.
- State that five retries still fail the latency target and that neither numeric threshold nor relevant comparability variables are recorded.
- Use a reader-visible evidence/authority register rather than asking the reader to infer the difference from prose.

## Composition Boundary

No explanation for the result difference, numeric margin, replacement configuration, or cross-environment conclusion is proposed. Those claims lack source support and remain deferred.
