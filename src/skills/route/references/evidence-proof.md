# Evidence Reuse And Proportional Proof

## Evidence References

Evidence remains owned by its authoritative source. Atlas stores a minimum disclosure-safe reference, not a copied claim. Record:

- exact source/evidence locator, producer, kind, and observation time;
- subject and original question;
- source revision/build/data/environment/baseline and relevant configuration;
- original method/result and limitations;
- candidate consumer and exact obligation for which reuse is proposed;
- invalidators and current disposition `clear | triggered | unknown`;
- evidence-appropriate freshness basis;
- applicability analysis, remaining gap, revalidation boundary/owner, and resulting fresh evidence locator;
- audience and sensitivity limits.

Assess reuse as `reusable_input | invalid | unknown`, never as inherited acceptance or a fresh pass. Reused evidence does not inherit authority, independence, completion, verification status, or a live-environment claim. Fake/conformer evidence cannot become live-provider evidence; writer proof cannot become independent proof; broad-suite success cannot silently satisfy a focused consumer endpoint. Revalidate only actual invalidators and remaining gaps—neither everything nor nothing by default.

A factual observation may apply an already accepted freshness, invalidator, or satisfaction rule only when initiated by an authenticated/scoped owning workflow or source authority and verified for source identity, environment, authorization, audience, provenance, and integrity. Failed verification remains `unknown`. An observation cannot amend accepted meaning, create a pass verdict, grant readiness, or establish Map completion.

## Proportional Proof Profile

Allocate proof by consequence, uncertainty, seams crossed, available independence, environment/effect needs, and inherited delivery Contracts. State why each gate is included or omitted.

1. **Focused Work Item proof:** writer-owned evidence at the deepest practical seam; exported seams prove immediate-consumer sufficiency.
2. **Independent integrated proof:** at meaningful Leg, Feature, and Map convergence, a checker independent from the implementing writer verifies the joined behavior against the named convergence Contract.
3. **Contextual Feature E2E:** each Feature names the real scenario appropriate to its ordinary PR/E2E boundary, environment, observations, cleanup, pass condition, and source owner. A small documentation/generator Feature may use real discovery/install smoke; mocks alone do not close a crossed real seam.
4. **Map joint proof:** one Map/Feature owner references, rather than copies, source evidence for cross-Feature or cross-Map outcomes.
5. **Early targeted security:** risky seams receive the smallest useful check before dependence accumulates, naming threat/trust assumption, evaluator, timing, and invalidator.
6. **Late full review when triggered:** after implementation stabilizes and before final real E2E, genuine feature delivery crossing privilege/authentication, secrets, payments, sensitive data, externally reachable interfaces, or material dependency/trust-boundary change receives full security review over the converged deliverable.
7. **Final real E2E where applicable:** after required focused/convergence/review gates, run the declared real scenario and observe final consumer behavior. Verified cleanup is part of pass for effectful E2E.

Include architecture, quality, or fidelity review only when consequence or inherited Contracts require it. Publisher and execution consumers cannot weaken or universally inflate the accepted allocation.

When independence or a real environment is unavailable, record the exact unavailable capability, affected claim, attempted source of independence, non-equivalent compensation, residual gap/forbidden claims, and later owner/trigger. Self-review, same-context repetition, fake providers, role names, or checkout isolation are not automatically independent.

Focused failures return to the owning Work Item when accepted design remains valid. Integrated failures return to their convergence/Feature. Strategy, boundary, dependency, or proof-design flaws require Route successor planning; responsibility/Contract flaws return to Blueprint; missing behavior returns to Frame/human authority. Production-code repair after a late review renews affected focused/convergence proof and the complete applicable review set before another final E2E. Test/environment correction resets only evidence it invalidated unless the trust boundary changed.

Proof obligations grant no credentials, network/provider access, spending, implementation, or deployment authority.
