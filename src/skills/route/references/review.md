# Review

Review challenges an ephemeral Map candidate; it does not own strategy, accepted meaning, publication, or implementation authority.

Classify feedback before applying it:

- **Conformance repair:** candidate contradicts accepted behavior, Blueprint, current Map Decision, or canonical Route guidance.
- **Evidence request:** improves proof for an existing candidate obligation without changing semantics.
- **Route proposal:** changes strategy, Feature/Leg/WI boundaries, prerequisites, convergence, migration, compatibility, cleanup, or proof design; revise and re-present the complete candidate.
- **Architecture finding:** responsibility, Contract, compatibility assumption, or invalidator is missing/wrong; return to Blueprint.
- **Behavior finding:** accepted intent is missing/contradictory; return to Frame/human reconciliation.
- **Governance proposal:** changes package membership, completion criteria, proof protocol, or review authority; keep advisory until separately accepted.
- **Mistaken/out of scope:** reject with reason.

Reviewer severity, repetition, consensus, agent identity, or a passing report cannot accept a Map or promote reused evidence. Review output stays advisory.

Challenge whether:

- the candidate is complete without Route scratch state;
- Features are coherent ordinary PR/E2E boundaries and Legs close integrated behavior rather than false slices;
- Work Items are bounded, Feature/Leg-owned, and exported seams prove immediate-consumer sufficiency;
- prerequisites are consumer-owned once, concrete, visibly acyclic in the bounded view, and converge under one named owner;
- Map coverage includes authority, identity/migration, compatibility, operations, publication recovery, cleanup, and cross-Map proof;
- the proof profile is proportional, independence limitations are honest, risky seams get early evidence, and late full security precedes final real E2E when triggered;
- evidence reuse preserves provenance, invalidators, remaining gaps, and scoped revalidation;
- the Publisher remains mechanical and implementation/effect authority remains absent.

A material correction produces a new fixed complete presentation. Review never patches an already accepted Decision; accepted semantic change uses a complete successor under the stable Map identity.
