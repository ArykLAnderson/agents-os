# Publication Readiness

External publication has two blocker classes:

- **Non-waivable publication invariant:** unsupported material assertions, stale support affecting the reader action, status or authority conflicts, missing material trace coverage, and untraced material visual or table assertions. Resolve these through the responsible operation and author-approved reconciliation when meaning changes. Human preference alone cannot authorize an external write past this invariant.
- **Human-overridable completion blocker:** a documented local or editorial limitation that does not violate the publication invariant. The human may accept it for the stated distribution boundary with a recorded reason.

A local artifact may remain a valid handoff when publication is blocked. Stage and release both enforce the non-waivable invariant.
