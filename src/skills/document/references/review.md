# Review

Select only lenses justified by current risk and representation:

- **Case fidelity:** claims, scope, authority, inference, disagreement, and support remain faithful to Cases.
- **Fresh-reader comprehension:** grounding, order, terminology, assumptions, and reader action work without hidden context.
- **Genre:** the selected genre's semantic obligations are fulfilled.
- **Editorial quality:** specificity, economy, coherence, voice, and claim strength fit the audience.
- **Presentation quality:** hierarchy, responsive layout, typography, visuals, links, attachments, and target behavior work in the rendered form.

Use fresh-context reviewers when available. Findings identify severity, exact location, consequence, evidence, and correction. Consolidate duplicates without averaging away disagreement.

Semantic findings return to compose, shape, trace, or Frame. Representation-only findings return to format. Re-run only affected lenses after material revision.

Review completes when material findings are resolved, accepted as limitations, or clearly require human judgment.
