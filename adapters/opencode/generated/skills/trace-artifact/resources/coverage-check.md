# Coverage Check

Inspect the artifact and its trace together after tracing.

Check that every material reader-facing assertion has one supported unit, every selected entry is represented by support or omission accounting, and every material table cell or row has an identified unit. A compact table does not inherit support from nearby prose.

Report each failure with the artifact anchor, missing or conflicting Case reference, severity, and disposition. These are trace blockers: unsupported decision or evidence; stale support; omitted material selected entry without accounting; material table or visual claim without a unit; or a Case-backed reader action unsupported by the Case. A supplied exercise action is not Case-backed: label it as external context and do not treat it as supported, blocked, or publishable.

Do not demand trace coverage for connective prose that makes no independent semantic assertion. This keeps the trace reviewable rather than sentence-by-sentence.
