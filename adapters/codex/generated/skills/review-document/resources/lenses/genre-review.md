# Genre Review Lens

Review whether the artifact works as the claimed genre for its named reader and action.

For an RFC decision brief, verify the decision or ask appears first, scope is bounded, supporting evidence and trade-offs are sufficient, risks and open gaps are visible, and the requested action is explicit. Flag a genre failure when a reader must reconstruct the recommendation from sources or when background crowds out the action.

Do not require a generic RFC outline. Recommend only changes that make the declared reader action possible without adding Case meaning.
